<p><b>Headhunting</b> is the method for players to obtain <a href="/wiki/Operator" title="Operator">Operators</a> in <i>Arknights: Endfield</i>. It is the game's core "gacha" system.
</p>

<p>As is standard fare for most gacha games, and to limit the adverse effects of its gambling, headhunting features "fail-safe" mechanisms that are colloquially referred to as <b>pity</b>:
</p>
<ol><li>A 5★ (at minimum) Operator is guaranteed to appear every ten pulls.
<ul><li>In other words, if a 6★ or 5★ Operator does not appear in nine pulls, then one will appear in the tenth pull. This means that at minimum, a 5★ Operator is guaranteed. This does not guarantee a 6★ Operator, however. A counter visible on the banner shows the remaining pulls until 5★ or higher Operator is guaranteed.</li></ul></li>
<li>If a 6★ Operator does not appear after 65 pulls, each subsequent pull there after will receive a 5% rate up boost. As such, the 6★ Operator rate will be 5.8% at the 66th pull.
<ul><li>The pull count and increased rate (a.k.a. "pity") will be reset as soon as a 6★ Operator appears on any pull, and is carried over across different headhunting banners.</li>
<li>If the player doesn't get a 6★ Operator after 79 pull, the 80th pull will guarantee a 6★ operator. This does not guarantee a rate up Operator, however.</li></ul></li>
<li>When obtaining a 6★ or 5★ Operator, there is a 50% chance to get the Rate up operator.</li>
<li>One time per banner, there's a guarantee system that if the player did not get the rate up 6★ Operator after 119 pull, the 120th pull is guaranteed to be the 6★ Operator on rate up. This does not carry over to other banners and will be reset when the banner ends.
<ul><li>Every 240th pull, a potential token of the 6★ Operator on rate up will be given. The number of pulls accounted will be reset when the banner ends.</li></ul></li></ol>
