<p><b>Arsenal Exchange</b> is a method for players to obtain <a href="/wiki/Weapon" title="Weapon">Weapons</a> in <i>Arknights: Endfield</i>. It is one of the game's core "gacha" systems.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview:_Arsenal_Exchange">Overview: Arsenal Exchange</span></h2>
<p>In the <b>Arsenal Exchange</b> the player can use <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_Ticket" title="Arsenal Ticket">Arsenal Tickets</a> (<span style="display:inline-block;"> <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_Ticket" title="Arsenal Ticket"><img alt="Arsenal Ticket icon.png" src="/images/thumb/Arsenal_Ticket_icon.png/22px-Arsenal_Ticket_icon.png?591ef0" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="22" height="22" data-file-width="84" data-file-height="84" /></a></span>) to exchange for supplies, mainly <a href="/wiki/Weapon" title="Weapon">weapons</a>.
</p><p>There are two ways to spend in Arsenal Exchange: Purchasing and Pulling.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Purchase_Weapons">Purchase Weapons</span></h3>
<p>Some weapons are put on sale permanently or rotates regularly; the amount of Arsenal Tickets required correspond to the rarity:
</p>
<ul><li>4★ weapons cost <span style="display:inline-block;">20 <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_Ticket" title="Arsenal Ticket"><img alt="Arsenal Ticket icon.png" src="/images/thumb/Arsenal_Ticket_icon.png/22px-Arsenal_Ticket_icon.png?591ef0" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="22" height="22" data-file-width="84" data-file-height="84" /></a></span></li>
<li>5★ weapons cost <span style="display:inline-block;">400 <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_Ticket" title="Arsenal Ticket"><img alt="Arsenal Ticket icon.png" src="/images/thumb/Arsenal_Ticket_icon.png/22px-Arsenal_Ticket_icon.png?591ef0" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="22" height="22" data-file-width="84" data-file-height="84" /></a></span></li>
<li>6★ weapons cost <span style="display:inline-block;">2480 <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_Ticket" title="Arsenal Ticket"><img alt="Arsenal Ticket icon.png" src="/images/thumb/Arsenal_Ticket_icon.png/22px-Arsenal_Ticket_icon.png?591ef0" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="22" height="22" data-file-width="84" data-file-height="84" /></a></span></li></ul>
<p>The list of weapons available regularly are:
</p>
<ul><li>A 6★ and a 5★ weapon are rotated weekly</li>
<li>Two 5★ and a 4★ weapons are rotated daily</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Pulling_Weapons">Pulling Weapons</span></h3>
<p>Weapons can be obtained through gacha. There are two types of banners known as <b>Issues</b>, one being rotating with the Operator's banner, and one being permanent. Each issue attempt costs <span style="display:inline-block;">1980 <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_Ticket" title="Arsenal Ticket"><img alt="Arsenal Ticket icon.png" src="/images/thumb/Arsenal_Ticket_icon.png/22px-Arsenal_Ticket_icon.png?591ef0" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="22" height="22" data-file-width="84" data-file-height="84" /></a></span> or, alternatively, an <b>Issue Cert</b> and gives 10 random weapons from the list of available weapons, with the following rates:
</p>

<th>Rarity

<p>Issuing features "fail-safe" mechanisms:
</p>
<ol><li>At least one 5★ or better weapon is guaranteed to appear in any of the issues.
<ul><li>In other words, if the first nine weapons are not 5★ or 6★ weapons, then the tenth weapon will be 5★ at minimum. This does not guarantee a 6★ Weapon, however.</li>
<li>If you don't get a 6★ weapon after 3 consecutive issues, the next issue will guarantee a 6★ weapon. This does not guarantee a Rate-UP weapon.</li></ul></li>
<li>When obtaining a 6★ weapon, there is a 25% chance to get the Rate-UP Weapon.</li>
<li>One time per banner, there's a guarantee system that if you did not get the Rate-UP 6★ weapon after 7 consecutive issues, the Rate-UP 6★ weapon is guaranteed in the 8th issue. This does not carry over to other issue banners and will be reset when the issue ends.
<ul><li>At the 10th issue, one weapon selection box will be given. At the 18th issue, the Rate-UP 6★ weapon will be given instead. The cycle follows on every 8 issues afterwards. The number of issues accounted will be reset when the issue ends.</li></ul></li></ol>
